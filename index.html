What is AlohaSafe Alert?

AlohaSafe Alert is a free, voluntary app service aimed at slowing the spread of COVID-19. It is the State of Hawaii’s official exposure notification app and has been developed in partnership with the Hawaii State Department of Health.

Who We Are?

We are a group of medical and computer science students at the University of Hawai'i hoping to increase the use of AlohaSafe while promoting the small businesses on our island.
